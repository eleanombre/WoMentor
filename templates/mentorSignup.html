-offer:

To consider before tweeting

What you can -offer could include:

Career advice:

Work on CV

Interview prep

Advice about the industry

Technical advice:

Specific questions

Teaching code

And also:

Networking

Providing resources

State everything you can -offer

We ask that mentors state everything that they can offer, and mentors state the top three things they are looking for.
That way, we can match up mentors with mentors who are best suited for their needs.

Split your tweets

If you have a lot to say, you can split your content into two tweets.
Just make sure you end them with "x/2".
