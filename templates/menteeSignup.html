Tips and tricks:

Split your tweets:

If you have a lot to say, you can split your content into two tweets: just end your tweet with "1/2" or equivalent.

State your top three picks:

We ask that mentors state everything that they can offer, and mentees state the top three things they are looking for -- that way, we can match up mentees with mentors who are best suited for their needs.
